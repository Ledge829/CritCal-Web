Tips

  • The player must have their Character Showcase enabled in Genshin Impact's in-game settings.
  • UIDs are 6-9 digit numbers — you can find yours in-game on your profile page.
  • Characters not in the showcase won't appear. Players can showcase up to 8 characters at a time.
  • Your recent UID searches are saved locally — they never leave your device.
