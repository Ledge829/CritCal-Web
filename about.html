The Project

About CritCal

A transparent build rater for Genshin Impact, built in the open by players, for players.

108 Characters
190+ Weapons
55 Sets

What it does

CritCal takes your character's stats, weapon, and artifacts, and gives you a tier-aware rating: a letter grade, a breakdown of where the score comes from, and specific recommendations for what to improve next. No black box -- every number on the results page is explained.

How scoring works

Weapons and artifact sets are scored against curated Best-in-Slot, Secondary, F2P, and Niche picks for each character. Anything outside that list still gets a fair score based on rarity and whether it's a real, recognized item -- an uncatalogued pick is never treated as a mistake.

Also on Discord

CritCal started as a Discord bot and still runs one alongside this site. Same scoring engine, same API, just a different way to reach it.

Built by the community

CritCal is open source and maintained by a single developer with community input on character data and priorities -- not a company, not a product with ads or a growth target. Just a tool meant to be useful.
